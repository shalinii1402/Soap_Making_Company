Our Story

Handcrafted with love, inspired by nature

How It All Began

Pure Essence was born from a simple belief: what we put on our skin should be as pure and natural as what we put in our bodies.

It started in a small kitchen in 2018, when founder Sarah Mitchell began making soap for her family after her daughter developed sensitive skin reactions to commercial products. What began as a solution for one family quickly grew into a passion for creating the finest handmade soaps using only natural, ethically sourced ingredients.

Today, Pure Essence has grown from that small kitchen to a dedicated workshop, but our commitment remains the same: every bar of soap is still handcrafted in small batches, using traditional cold-process methods that preserve the natural benefits of our ingredients.
