Botanical Ingredients

Committed to 100% transparency. Every single ingredient has a purpose.

Nature is our Laboratory

We believe that nature provides everything we need for healthy, glowing skin. Our ingredients are chosen for their specific therapeutic properties and sustainability.

Base Oils & Butters

Coconut Oil

Source: Organic Coconut Farms

Known for its exceptional cleansing properties, it produces a rich, fluffy lather and provides a protective barrier for the skin.

Olive Oil

Source: Mediterranean Olive Groves

Extremely gentle and moisturizing. Rich in Vitamin E and antioxidants, it nourishes and softens the skin without clogging pores.

Shea Butter

Source: Ethical Women's Cooperatives in Africa

A powerhouse of moisturization. High concentrations of fatty acids and vitamins make shea butter ideal for softening skin.

Pure Essential Oils

Lavender & Botanicals

Source: Pure Steam-distilled Flowers

Renowned for calming and relaxing aromas. These pure extracts provide natural fragrance and therapeutic skin benefits.
